ABOUT THE SITE

This fitness blog is a compilation of my 124 days of my PE 2217 AIKIDO COURSE Journey.

Aikido is all about the arts and spirit that make the Aikido being brought to life, which made me understand its purpose and what it wants me to carve my path in my life.

In Aikido, I have found my duties and fully discovered where I am capable of, especially from the mistakes that we can't change. But instead, we learn from our wrongdoings.

Though it is a form of martial arts, we are not destined to attack but about to

defend ourselves, imploring our hearts and strengthening the positivity in life.

RAJ DANIEL OCAMPO
